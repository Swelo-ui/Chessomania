About & Rules

A state-of-the-art, fully offline chess application. Designed to be resource-friendly, ChessOmania operates entirely in your browser with zero external APIs, ensuring absolute privacy and instant gameplay.

Engine: ChessOmania Local Stockfish (WASM)
Piece Themes: 42 custom sets (Lichess)
Board Themes: 19 unique styles (Wood, Marble, etc.)
Sounds: 10 selectable sound themes
RAM Footprint: ~12MB (Lightweight!)
Network Connection: 100% Offline / No Internet Required

How Pieces Move

King (♔): 1 square in any direction.
Queen (♕): Multi-directional, any distance.
Rook (♖): Horizontally/vertically, any distance.
Bishop (♗): Diagonally, any distance.
Knight (♘): L-shape (2+1), jumps over pieces.
Pawn (♙): 1 square forward. Captures diagonally.

Special Rules

  • Castling: Move King 2 squares toward Rook for safety (neither must have moved).
  • En Passant: Capture enemy pawn that just skipped past your pawn by moving 2 squares.
  • Promotion: Reach 8th rank to promote Pawn to Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight.
  • Checkmate: King is under attack and cannot escape. Game over!
